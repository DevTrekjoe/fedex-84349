Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my FedEx package?
You can track your package by entering your tracking number on our tracking page. You'll find real-time updates on your shipment's location and estimated delivery date. You can also track via the FedEx mobile app or by calling 1-800-463-3339.
How do I schedule a pickup?
Log in to your FedEx account and navigate to "Schedule a Pickup." You can choose a one-time or recurring pickup at your location. Pickups can be scheduled for the same day (before cutoff) or future dates. There may be a fee depending on your account type.
What are the size and weight limits for FedEx packages?
FedEx Express and FedEx Ground packages can weigh up to 150 lbs. Maximum dimensions are 108" in length and 165" in length plus girth. For larger shipments, consider FedEx Freight for LTL (less-than-truckload) options.
How do I file a claim for a lost or damaged package?
You can file a claim online through your FedEx account or by calling 1-800-463-3339. Claims for damage must be filed within 60 days and claims for loss within 9 months. You'll need your tracking number, proof of value, and photos of damage if applicable.
Can I redirect a package after it's been shipped?
Yes, with FedEx Delivery Manager you can redirect packages, request to hold at a FedEx location, provide delivery instructions, or schedule a specific delivery date. Sign up for free at fedex.com to access these features.
What is FedEx Hold at Location?
FedEx Hold at Location allows you to have your package held at a nearby FedEx office, Walgreens, Dollar General, or other partner location for pickup at your convenience. You can set this up through FedEx Delivery Manager or by contacting customer service.
How do I open a FedEx account?
You can open a FedEx account online for free at fedex.com. Business accounts provide access to discounted rates (up to 45% off), invoice billing, account management tools, and detailed shipping reports. Visit our sign up page to get started.
